MathxPlay Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2025

1. Introduction

Thank you for using MathxPlay. This privacy policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

Data We Collect Directly:

  • Device information (model, operating system version) for app compatibility
  • Basic usage statistics to improve app performance
  • Game progress and scores (stored locally on your device)

Data Collected by Third Parties:

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect:

  • Advertising ID (unique identifier for advertising purposes)
  • Device information (device type, operating system, screen resolution)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • App usage data (session duration, app interactions)
  • Ad interaction data (clicks, views, impressions)

3. How We Use Information

The information is used for:

  • Ensuring the app runs smoothly on your device
  • Saving your game progress locally
  • Identifying and fixing technical issues
  • Improving user experience
  • Displaying relevant advertisements (through AdMob)

4. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to serve advertisements in our app. AdMob may use your data to:

  • Show personalized ads based on your interests
  • Measure ad performance
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

For more information about Google's data practices, please visit:

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google AdMob Privacy: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

5. Information Sharing

  • We do not share your personal data with third parties for commercial purposes
  • Google AdMob processes data as described in their privacy policy
  • Data may be shared with authorized authorities when required by law
  • Anonymous, aggregated data may be shared for analytical purposes

6. Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Restriction: Request limitation of data processing
  • Data Portability: Request transfer of your data
  • Object: Object to data processing
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw your consent at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us at: sadikahmetbusiness@gmail.com

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our app and advertising partners may use:

  • Cookies for ad serving and measurement
  • Device identifiers for advertising purposes
  • Analytics tools for app performance monitoring

You can manage your advertising preferences through your device settings.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Game progress is stored locally on your device
  • We do not store personal information on our servers
  • All data transmission is encrypted where applicable
  • We regularly review our security practices

9. International Data Transfers

Some of your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your jurisdiction. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers, including:

  • Adequacy decisions by relevant authorities
  • Standard contractual clauses
  • Other lawful transfer mechanisms

10. Data Retention

  • Local game data: Retained until you uninstall the app
  • Analytics data: Retained for up to 26 months
  • Advertising data: Retention periods are controlled by Google AdMob

11. Children's Privacy

MathxPlay is suitable for users of all ages, including children. We are committed to protecting children's privacy:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • All game progress is stored locally on the device
  • Parents can contact us regarding their child's data
  • We comply with applicable children's privacy laws (COPPA, GDPR-K)

12. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For advertising (Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy)
  • Google Play Services: For app functionality

Each service has its own privacy policy governing their data practices.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated through:

  • App update notifications on Google Play Store
  • In-app notifications
  • Email notifications (if contact information is available)

We encourage you to review this policy regularly.

14. Contact

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, you can contact us at: sadikahmetbusiness@gmail.com
