MathxPlay Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2025

1. Introduction

Thank you for using MathxPlay. This privacy policy explains how your data is collected, used, and protected when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

Our application collects minimal information necessary for functionality:

  • Device information (model, operating system version) for app compatibility
  • Basic usage statistics to improve app performance
  • Game progress and scores (stored locally on your device)

3. How We Use Information

The collected information is used for the following purposes:

  • To ensure the app runs smoothly on your device
  • To save your game progress locally
  • To identify and fix any technical issues
  • To make the app more user-friendly

4. Information Sharing

Your personal data is not shared with third parties. However, it may be shared with authorized authorities when required by law.

5. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. All game progress and scores are stored locally on your device. We do not store any personal information on our servers. The app only requires basic device information to function properly.

6. Children's Privacy

MathxPlay is an educational application suitable for users of all ages, including children. We are committed to protecting the privacy of all our users, including children. We do not collect any personal information from our users, and all game progress is stored locally on the device.

7. Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be notified through the app's update notes on the Google Play Store. We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, you can contact us at sadikahmetbusiness@gmail.com.
